Where can I learn more about health care quality?

If you are interested in learning more about quality care or how to choose a hospital, nursing home or doctor, here are some resources that can help.

Agency for Healthcare Research & Quality (AHRQ)

  • Consumers & Patients provides more links to tools and information for people who want to take an active role in their health care.

To contact AHRQ by mail write to:

Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality

Hospital Compare - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services

  • Hospital Compare is a website with quality information on almost all hospitals in the US.
  • The Hospital Compare website’s Hospital Checklist helps you think through a range of issues to consider when choosing a hospital.

Physician Compare

Physician Compare can help you find a physician nearby. You can search by specialty, board certification, group practice, and which hospitals the physician works with, among other topics.

Nursing Home Compare

Nursing Home Compare collects information about every nursing home in the country that provides care to people who receive Medicare or Medicaid benefits. The website also offers a detailed guide to choosing a nursing home.
